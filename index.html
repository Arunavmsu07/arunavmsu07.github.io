About

Research & Expertise

I am a doctoral researcher in Biosystems Engineering and Civil and Environmental Engineering at Michigan State University. My work integrates NASA SMAP and Copernicus Sentinel-1 satellite products to characterize soil moisture dynamics, optimize soil hydraulic properties, and map global irrigation patterns at high spatiotemporal resolution.

I have contributed to NASA's SMAPVEX22 field validation campaign, published in Agricultural Water Management and Frontiers in Environmental Science, and delivered presentations at AGU, IGARSS, and ASABE. I hold an M.Tech from the Indian Institute of Remote Sensing (IIRS-ISRO) and a B.Tech from Orissa University of Agriculture and Technology.
